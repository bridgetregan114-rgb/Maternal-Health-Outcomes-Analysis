# Data Inventory

## Dataset
CDC WONDER Natality

## Source
CDC WONDER

## Geographic Level
State

## Time Period
2023

## Number of Records
51
(50 states, Puerto Rico)

## Key Variables
- State or Territory of Residence
- Average Age of Mother (years)
- Average Pre-pregnancy BMI
- Average Number of Prenatal Visits
- Average Interval Since Last Live Birth (months)
- Average Interval Since Last Other Pregnancy Outcome (months)

## Potential Use
This dataset will be used to identify maternal demographic and healthcare-related factors that may be 
associated with maternal health outcomes across U.S. states.

Potential predictor variables include:
- Maternal age
- Pre-pregnancy BMI
- Prenatal care utilization
- Birth spacing

## Limitations
- Does not directly measure maternal mortality.
- Does not include healthcare provider availability.
- Does not include insurance coverage information.
- Does not include socioeconomic variables such as income or poverty.
- Additional datasets will be required to evaluate maternal health outcomes and explanatory factors.
- Data is limited to 2023 observations.

##Questions Raised
- Is maternal age associated with variation in maternal health outcomes across states?
- Is prenatal care utilization associated with improved maternal health outcomes?
- Does pre-pregnancy BMI vary significantly across states?
- What additional datasets are needed to measure maternal health outcomes directly?
